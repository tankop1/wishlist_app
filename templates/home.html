About Us

Too many times, we say we will take that trip and make those memories “someday.” Our question for you is, why wait? Start dreaming now of the vacations you want to take, and we at Wish List Travel will help turn your travel wishes into memories.

Whether you want to take a photography safari in Africa, a cruise in the Bahamas, or a river tour in Europe, we can help make that a reality. No travel dream is out of reach! Wish List Travel is an all-inclusive travel agency that can book any form of transportation, accommodations, tours, and attractions across the globe. What’s more, we are big believers in education. Our agents have certifications from many of our vendors, including our owner being a Certified Cruise Counsellor. We use that knowledge to help make your perfect travel fantasies come true. Don’t stop at just hoping.

Register with us and we can start looking for the best deals and packages in the locales of your dreams. No dream vacation is out of reach. Let us make your travel wishes a reality!
